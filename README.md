## What problem does Fretboard Lab solve?

Most chord sites show one shape. Real arranging, teaching, and songwriting need options: smaller stretches, smoother voice leading, open-string color, alternate tunings, and printable diagrams that fit the player.

## The result

Use Fretboard Lab when you need a concrete result, not another generic tool window:

- Chord and progression exploration across the fretboard.
- Difficulty, position, span, and open-string sorting.
- Standard and alternate tuning workflows.
- Roman progression navigation for harmony study.

## Who it is for

- Guitar teachers building clear lesson sheets and handouts.
- Songwriters and arrangers exploring voicing choices instead of memorizing one grip.
- Players learning harmony, Roman progressions, and fretboard geography through playable shapes.

## Product capabilities
